About Me

Haniya Amjad is a Product Manager who partners with business stakeholders and end-users to translate ambiguous problems into clear, actionable product features. She works across Slack channels, Gmail threads, and Google Meet sessions to gather requirements and distil problem statements into well-scoped opportunities.

Her approach centres on rapid prototyping. She uses Figma and AI-powered design tools to generate interactive mockups within hours, then presents these prototypes alongside engineering teams to align on scope, track performance, and iterate continuously. The goal is always the same: reduce ambiguity, accelerate decisions, and build the right thing.

Before her current role, Haniya built experience across cross-functional product teams, bridging the gap between business stakeholders, design, and engineering. She is passionate about user-centred product development and the intersection of rapid experimentation with disciplined delivery.
